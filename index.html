What Is PFF?

A geosocial networking application that allows users to interact based on matching shared interests.

group

Is PFF For You?

Sometimes, all you need is a simple way to meet like-minded people in your area, PFF matches people based on their shared interests. Whether you like to attend major-league baseball games or go wine-tasting, through PFF you will be able to find your Platonic Friends Forever.

How It All Works!

We use similar technologies as major dating and social newtworking companies in order to connect people. Our intention is to improve the way people connect with others for a platonic campanionship, creating a new meaning to 'friends with benefits'.
